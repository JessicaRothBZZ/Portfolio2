About

We are a specialist shop for creative and delicate cake design in Zurich. We create your wedding cake, festival cake and dessert `s in the finest Swiss confectioner quality. Traditional, modern, romantic, colourful, imaginative, flowery, made of chocolate, or decorated with gold - for the sugary crowning of your celebration, we will fulfill all your cake dreams.

All decorations like sugar flowers, ornaments and ornaments, are handmade by us. Only this handicraft art makes every wedding cake and celebration cake unique. Your wishes and ideas will be realized by us absolutely reliable and carefully.

Have we aroused your interest? We would be pleased to invite you to a personal consultation and tasting at our studio in Zurich. Our opening hours for tastings and cake meetings are from Mo. to Fr. 08.00 to 18.30, Saturdays from 08.00 to 12.00. By appointment only.

Wedding cakes

Description

The crowning glory of any wedding dinner is without doubt the wedding cake. We guarantee for innovative creations and customer proximity. That is why we strive to create cakes that meet the bridal couple's wishes in taste and shape, so that no personal customer wish remains unfulfilled. Every wedding cake is made for you by our specialists with the utmost care and an eye for detail. We are happy to offer you a free consultation with tasting.

Delivery

Order the cake early, ideally 3 weeks to 3 months in advance. We deliver our cakes throughout Switzerland directly to the refrigerated room of the hotel/restaurant of your party. Wedding cakes and festive cakes can also be collected from us personally. For this purpose we have special cool boxes for a smooth transport.
